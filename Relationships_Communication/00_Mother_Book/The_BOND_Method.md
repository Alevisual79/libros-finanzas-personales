## Prologue

Elena has been prioritizing her career over her relationships for years. Now she has professional success and a void she cannot name.

Ruben knows how to sell ideas to clients but cannot communicate with the people who matter most to him.

Isabel has spent thirty years managing people at work and just realized she does not really know her own children.

Three capable, functional and lonely people in ways they do not admit in public. The BOND Method is for them.
