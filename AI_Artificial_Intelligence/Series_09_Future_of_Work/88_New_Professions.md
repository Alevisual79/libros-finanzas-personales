## Prologue

Elena's job title, 'AI Systems Integration Architect', did not exist five years ago. The skills it required were real and valuable. The profession had emerged because the technology had emerged. She wondered what the next generation of professions would look like.

## Epilogue

New professions emerge when new technology creates new problems that require human expertise to solve. AI is creating more new problems faster than any technology in history. The professions of 2030 are being designed right now — mostly without a roadmap.
