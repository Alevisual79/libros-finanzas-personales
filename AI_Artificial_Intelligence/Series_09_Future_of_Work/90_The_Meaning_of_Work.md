## Prologue

What do you do?' is the first question at every social gathering. For many people, work is identity. When AI can do significant parts of that work — better, faster, cheaper — the question becomes uncomfortable. This is not just a productivity challenge. It is an existential one.

## Epilogue

Work has always been more than economic exchange. It is the arena where most adults find structure, competence, connection, and contribution. AI changes the work. It does not change the human need for those things. Finding them in the new landscape is the project.
