## Prologue

Elena's 12-year-old cousin had better AI tool skills than most adults she worked with. He had no understanding of how any of it worked. The skills were real. The literacy was absent. The combination is dangerous.

## Epilogue

AI literacy is not the same as AI skill. Skills without literacy produce confident misuse. The goal of AI education is not AI users — it is AI citizens: people who can use AI tools and understand their implications.
