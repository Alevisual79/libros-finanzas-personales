## Prologue

The paradox of the hyper-connected era: more tools for communication than any previous generation, higher rates of reported loneliness than any previous measurement. AI adds another layer to a problem that is not primarily a technology problem.

## Epilogue

AI cannot solve loneliness. Loneliness is a signal that human connection is missing. AI can mimic connection temporarily. The solution is investing in the conditions for real human relationships — a problem that requires social, not technical, solutions.
