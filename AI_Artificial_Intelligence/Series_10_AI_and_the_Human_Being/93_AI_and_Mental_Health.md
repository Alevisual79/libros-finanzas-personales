## Prologue

Woebot launched as a mental health chatbot in 2017. Millions of people found it useful. Some found it transformative. Critics worried about the replacement of professional care by an algorithm. The evidence was more nuanced than either the enthusiasm or the alarm.

## Epilogue

AI mental health tools can extend access to support for people who would otherwise go without. They cannot replace the therapeutic relationship, the clinical judgment, and the human presence that is at the core of effective treatment. The best models integrate both.
