## Preface: Three People, One Realization

Elena, 34, has a gym membership she uses twice a year. Ruben, 29, eats when he remembers and sleeps when the work stops. Isabel, 52, has been ignoring her annual checkup for three years and managing chronic stress with coffee and willpower.

All three are functional. None are thriving. The RESET Method changes that.

## Chapter 1: The RESET Framework

**R — Reconnect:** Understand your relationship with food, movement, and sleep.
**E — Eliminate:** Remove the habits that are silently undermining your health.
**S — Sustain:** Build routines that continue without constant willpower.
**E — Evolve:** Adapt your health practices as your life and body change.
**T — Thrive:** Move beyond absence of illness to genuine vitality.

---

## Chapter 2: What Health Actually Means

Health is not the absence of illness. It is the presence of energy, resilience, and functional capacity. The RESET Method defines health as your ability to do what matters to you — today, next year, and twenty years from now.
