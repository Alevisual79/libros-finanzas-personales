## Prologue

Isabel eliminated carbohydrates after reading a popular book. Her energy crashed, her athletic performance declined, and her mood deteriorated. The evidence does not support broad carbohydrate elimination for most people.

Carbohydrates are not the enemy. Refined, rapidly absorbed carbohydrates in excess are the issue. Whole food carbohydrates, timed appropriately, are an effective fuel source for most people.
