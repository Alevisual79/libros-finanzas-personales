# The Comparison Trap
## How to Stop Measuring Your Worth Against Others

**Series 1: Mindset and Beliefs — Book 6 of 10**

## Prologue

Elena's benchmark was always her most accomplished colleague. Every achievement she reached, she moved the benchmark. The chase for a moving target made every success feel insufficient.
