## Prologue

Carol Dweck's research on mindset has been misunderstood as 'believe you can improve'. The actual insight is harder: it requires genuinely finding value in effort, feedback, and difficulty — even when they are uncomfortable.

## Epilogue

The growth mindset is not optimism. It is a specific orientation toward difficulty: not 'this will work out' but 'this difficulty is information, and I will learn from it.'
