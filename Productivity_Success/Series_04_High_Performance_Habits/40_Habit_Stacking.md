# Habit Stacking
## How to Build New Habits in the Cracks of Existing Ones

**Series 4: High Performance Habits — Book 10 of 10**

## Prologue

The hardest part of building a new habit is not the habit itself — it is finding a slot in an already-full routine. Habit stacking solves this by anchoring new behaviors to existing automatic ones.
