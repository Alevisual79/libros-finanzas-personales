# The Weekly Review
## The Practice That Keeps Everything Else on Track

**Series 4: High Performance Habits — Book 9 of 10**

---

## Prologue

Isabel's version of the weekly review: every Friday at 4pm she closed her laptop, cleared her physical desk, and spent 45 minutes reviewing the week and planning the next. In a decade of this practice, she had missed fewer than ten sessions.
