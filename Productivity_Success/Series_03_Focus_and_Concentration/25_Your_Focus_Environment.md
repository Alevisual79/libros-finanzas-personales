**Series 3: Focus and Concentration — Book 5 of 10**

## Prologue

Ruben's home office was set up for convenience, not concentration. His desk faced a window with a view of the street. His phone charged at his desk. His messaging apps were always open. Each choice was individually small. Together they made sustained focus nearly impossible.
