# The 5-Hour Workday
## Productivity Without More Hours

**Series 2: Time Management — Book 10 of 10**

## Prologue

Research on knowledge workers consistently shows that focused productive work rarely exceeds five hours per day — and that extending hours past that point produces diminishing returns and accelerating errors. The question is not how long to work, but how well.
