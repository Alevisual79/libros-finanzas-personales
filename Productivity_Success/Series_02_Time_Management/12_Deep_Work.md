# Deep Work
## How to Do Your Most Important Work in the Distracted World

**Series 2: Time Management — Book 2 of 10**

## Prologue

Cal Newport coined the term 'deep work' to describe the cognitively demanding, focused work that creates most of the value in knowledge work. It is also the work that the modern workplace is most systematically designed to prevent.
