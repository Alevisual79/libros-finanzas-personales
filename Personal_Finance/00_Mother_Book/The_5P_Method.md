## Preface: Three People and Money

Elena earns well and saves little. Ruben freelances and has no financial safety net. Isabel is 52 and has been putting off thinking seriously about retirement for a decade.

All three are functional financially. None are in control of their financial futures. The 5P Method changes that.

---

## Chapter 1: The 5P Framework

**P1 — Psychology:** Understand your emotional relationship with money before trying to change your behavior.
**P2 — Protection:** Build the financial floor that prevents a setback from becoming a catastrophe.
**P3 — Plan:** Create a spending, saving, and investment plan that matches your actual life.
**P4 — Position:** Build assets that work for you while you work for them.
**P5 — Purpose:** Align your financial decisions with what you actually want from your life.
